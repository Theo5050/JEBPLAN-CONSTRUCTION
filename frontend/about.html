Our Story

Founded by Justice Essoun Baah

2020 - Present

jebplan Construction was established in 2020 by Justice Essoun Baah with a clear vision: to provide high-quality construction materials and services to the Ghanaian market. Starting from Kwabenya, Accra, we have grown to become a reliable name in scaffold supply, security doors, and construction accessories.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction. We don't just supply materials; we build lasting relationships with our clients through reliable service and expert advice.
